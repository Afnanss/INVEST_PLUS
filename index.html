Frequently Asked Questions

What is the INVEST+ Card?

+

Invest+ is a smart and innovative investment feature offered by SNB that simplifies the way customers grow their wealth. It automatically opens and manages an investment wallet for you, allowing you to invest effortlessly without any complex setup. Every time you use your SNB card, a portion of your spending is directed into investments, making saving and investing a seamless part of your daily life. Designed for all customers, regardless of their experience level, Invest+ provides secure, easy-to-use tools and exceptional customer service, helping you achieve your financial goals with confidence and convenience.

Who is eligible for the INVEST+ Card?

+

The INVEST+ Card is available to all adults customers with no complicated requirements, making it inclusive and accessible.

How does the investment wallet work?

+

The investment wallet in Invest+ automatically collects a portion of your spending every time you swipe your SNB card, securely allocates it into diversified investments managed by experts, and allows you to track or withdraw your funds anytime through the SNB app.

What are the benefits of using the INVEST+ Card?

+

The INVEST+ Card not only helps grow your wealth by automatically investing a portion of your spending but also offers exclusive benefits such as discounts at partner merchants and complimentary access to premium airport lounges, combining financial growth with luxury and convenience.
